# Use Cases

## Internal Scientist Briefing

Who uses it:

- DMPK scientists
- ADME scientists
- project teams preparing early strategy discussions

Why it is valuable:

- it consolidates chemistry, literature, and assay reasoning into one report
- it helps teams discuss why certain pathways or assays should be prioritized

Recommended report mode:

- `scientist`

## Executive Portfolio Review

Who uses it:

- project leads
- portfolio managers
- non-specialist decision stakeholders

Why it is valuable:

- it reduces technical detail into top priorities, main risks, and immediate next steps
- it helps non-specialists understand what should happen next without reading the full technical stack

Recommended report mode:

- `executive`

## CRO Discussion Support

Who uses it:

- internal teams preparing external assay discussions
- teams scoping early work packages with CRO partners

Why it is valuable:

- it reframes the analysis as a suggested assay package
- it distinguishes first-pass experiments from confirmatory and exploratory follow-up

Recommended report mode:

- `cro_proposal`

## Early Regulatory-Prep Discussion

Who uses it:

- internal teams preparing conservative evidence summaries
- teams organizing early risk, uncertainty, and translation discussions

Why it is valuable:

- it makes confidence caveats and evidence boundaries explicit
- it separates what is supported now from what remains plausible but unverified

Recommended report mode:

- `regulatory_prep`
