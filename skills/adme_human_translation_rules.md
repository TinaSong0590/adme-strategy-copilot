# ADME Human Translation Rules

## Translation Priorities

- Use microsomal and hepatocyte data to frame IVIVE-based hepatic clearance estimates
- Compare metabolite profiles across species before drawing human relevance conclusions
- If CYP3A liability is suspected, evaluate DDI risk early
- If metabolic turnover is high, confirm whether low exposure is driven by clearance, solubility, or bioavailability
