# ADME Preclinical Design

Use this skill to prioritize a compact but professional discovery ADME package.

## Core Study Stack

- Human liver microsomes for intrinsic clearance and metabolite soft spot discovery
- S9 fraction for mixed Phase I and Phase II metabolism
- Cryopreserved hepatocytes for intact-cell metabolism and cross-species comparison
- Plasma stability to assess parent stability outside the liver
- CYP phenotyping when oxidative liability or DDI risk is suspected

## In Vivo Defaults

- Use the requested species as the primary exploratory PK species
- Recommend dense early sampling to capture rapid turnover
- Collect plasma and, when possible, excreta pools for route-of-clearance insight
